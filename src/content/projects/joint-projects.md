Some of our work is carried out hand in hand with Rotary clubs, subgroups and partner organisations around the world. These joint projects bring together the skills and generosity of many to reach children and families who would otherwise go without care.

## Heart2Heart

Many children with life-threatening heart conditions face dire consequences while they wait. At Heart2Heart we treat Egyptian children with congenital heart disease using catheter procedures — 100% free.

These children need your support. Together we can give them the chance of a healthy life.
